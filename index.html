Why Cats Make Great Pets

Cats are independent, playful, and loving companions. Here’s why people adore them:

  • They are independent but enjoy affection.
  • Low-maintenance compared to other pets.
  • Curious, playful, and endlessly entertaining.

Cat Behavior & Traits

Cats have fascinating behaviors and unique personalities:

  • Purring when happy or relaxed.
  • Sleeping for long stretches throughout the day.
  • Exploring and being naturally curious.
